Evocation

The game follows a lonely girl as she traverses the spirit realm after recently passing away. Follow her as she meets unusual beings and tries to achieve her goal of recovering her memories to resolve how she passed. Will she choose peace or want revenge for her death?

I was the Lead Programmer in this project, I was in charge of the level programming and the incorporation and adjustment of the assets provided by the artists.
